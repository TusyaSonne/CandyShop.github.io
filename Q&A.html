Frequently Asked Questions

What are your payment methods?

We accept payment by bank cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) and electronic payment systems (PayPal, Apple Pay). Also when if desired, you can pay the courier in cash upon delivery or using the receipt.

What is your delivery time and cost?

We provide free shipping for orders over 2000 rubles. For orders below this amount, the shipping cost will depend on your locations. The delivery time is usually from 3 to 7 business days in depending on your location.

Do you have wholesale prices for business customers?

Yes, we have special wholesale prices for business customers. Contact us for more information about our wholesale offers.

What quality guarantees do you provide?

We guarantee the quality of all our products. If you have any problems With the quality of the product, please contact our customer support team to resolve the issue.

Can I return or exchange the product?

We accept refunds and exchanges within 30 days of purchase, provided that The product has not been unpacked and has its original packaging preserved. Details information about the return and exchange of goods can be found in the "Return Policy" section of our website.

What are the features of storing your sweets?

For optimal preservation of the freshness of our sweets, it is recommended to store them in in a cool and dry place at a temperature of 15°C to 20°C, away from direct sunlight. Ice cream should be stored at a temperature from 0°C to -20°C, without contact with foreign products.

Do you have any dietary products?

At the moment, we do not have an assortment that would suit people with dietary needs. restrictions. You can always contact us and tell us about your offers, or order something specific from our couriers.

Where do your ingredients come from?

We carefully select our ingredients from reliable suppliers from all over the world to to ensure the high quality of our sweets. We strive to use natural and high-quality components to provide our customers with maximum enjoyment from sweets.
